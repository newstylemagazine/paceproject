## 

Narrative | March 23, 2021

In 2019, we launched TRaCE McGill: a project which tracked over 4500 PhDs who graduated from McGill between 2008 and 2018. We’ve completed our interviewing, and have now published over 100 narratives from PhD graduates across disciplines.

Visit the TRaCE McGill website at www.tracemcgill.com to read new narratives, access our events, and subscribe to our newsletter.
